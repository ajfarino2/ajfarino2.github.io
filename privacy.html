Privacy Policy for TuneTransit

Effective Date: August 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Anthony Farino ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use TuneTransit, including the mobile app, related extensions, and associated websites/APIs (such as share.tunetransit.app and core.tunetransit.app). By using TuneTransit, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process information needed to operate the App and deliver its features. Categories include:

  • Account & Permissions (optional): If you connect supported music services or grant media library access, we use the resulting tokens/permissions to perform actions you request. We never receive your service passwords.
  • Content You Provide: Music links you paste, enter, or share with the App; optional playlist names; and other information you submit while using features.
  • In‑App Usage: Items you convert or save, your local conversion history, and preferences you configure. This information is primarily stored on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you buy Premium, Apple’s StoreKit provides purchase status we need to unlock features. We do not receive or store your full payment details.
  • Device/Technical Information: Limited device and app information may be used to maintain security, prevent abuse, and operate certain features (for example, validating requests or applying fair‑use limits).
  • Clipboard Access: When you return to the App, we may check locally if your clipboard contains a music link to help you start faster. You can ignore the suggestion.
  • Last.fm (optional): If you opt in to Spotify “Now Playing” detection, the App may use your Last.fm username and a minimal credential to read your public now‑playing status and basic track metadata from Last.fm. We do not collect your Last.fm password.

Sources: We collect information directly from you (when you use features), from your device/OS (subject to permissions), and from third‑party services you choose to connect.

2. Purposes and Legal Bases

We use information to:

  • Provide core features you request (for example, opening content in supported services or creating playlists on your behalf when you ask us to).
  • Operate the App and related extensions, including saving your history locally and enabling sharing features you choose to use.
  • Verify and manage purchases (via Apple’s StoreKit) and determine access to Premium features.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud/abuse, and ensure fair use of free features.
  • Communicate with third‑party services only as needed to fulfill your requests in the App.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Last.fm (optional): When enabled, we request your now‑playing status and basic track details from Last.fm solely to display in the App and to improve music link detection for Spotify users. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or unrelated analytics.

Legal bases (where applicable): performance of a contract (core features you request), legitimate interests (security, fraud/abuse prevention, fair use), and consent (optional features like Last.fm, certain notifications, and clipboard checks).

Global Privacy Control/Do Not Track: We do not use your information for cross‑context behavioral advertising and honor applicable opt‑out rights under local law.

3. Sharing and Disclosures

We do not sell or “share” your personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising. We share information only as needed to operate the App, with:

  • Third‑party services you connect or request we use, to carry out actions you initiate in the App.
  • Apple services and frameworks (for example, In‑App Purchases, device‑level storage, and iCloud features you enable) to deliver functionality and securely store data on your device.
  • Service providers that support secure operation (for example, to validate requests or prevent abuse).
  • Authorities, if required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users and the public.
  • Smart Links (share.tunetransit.app): When you choose to generate a shareable link, a public URL is created so others can open your content in supported services. We include only the minimum details you provide (e.g., title, artist, optional artwork, destination links). Anyone with the URL can view the card. We may retain the link and minimal metadata for operations and abuse‑prevention. You can request removal of a specific link.
  • Last.fm (optional): If you enable the integration, we send read‑only requests to Last.fm to read your now‑playing status and related track metadata. We do not post to or modify your Last.fm account.

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize security and privacy by design:

  • On‑Device Storage: Most app data (including conversion history, saved items, and preferences) is stored on your device. Sensitive values are secured using system‑level storage. You can delete local data in the App and by uninstalling the App.
  • Cloud/Sync Features: If you enable features that rely on iCloud or similar Apple services, limited data (for example, counters used to manage free‑tier usage) may be stored within your private iCloud space and is handled under Apple’s terms.
  • Network Security: Data exchanged with third‑party services is transmitted over encrypted connections. We request only what is needed to perform the actions you ask for.
  • Last.fm Data: Your Last.fm username and any minimal credential used for the optional integration are stored securely on your device. We do not store your Last.fm password, and we do not maintain a server‑side copy of your listening history.

5. Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described:

  • On‑device data: Remains until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App.
  • Smart links: Persist while the link remains active; minimal metadata may be retained to prevent abuse. You can request removal of a specific link.
  • Optional integrations: Disabling Last.fm or removing your username stops requests; associated local data can be removed in Settings.
  • Security/abuse‑prevention logs: Retained for a limited period (typically no more than 30 days) and then deleted or anonymized.

6. International Transfers

We may process information in locations where we and our service providers operate. Where transfer safeguards are required, we rely on appropriate mechanisms provided by our vendors (for example, standard contractual clauses) and implement additional measures as appropriate.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Permissions: Grant or revoke permissions (such as media library access) at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Local Data: Clear history and saved items within the App. Deleting the App removes local data on your device.
  • Purchases: Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings. Use in‑app options to restore purchases.
  • Marketing: We do not use your information for unrelated marketing communications.
  • Last.fm Controls: Enable/disable the integration in Settings or revoke access from your Last.fm account.

Depending on your region, you may have additional rights (for example, access, deletion, correction, portability, restriction, and objection). You can exercise rights by contacting us; we may need to verify your request.

8. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell or share personal information as defined by California law. You have the right to know, delete, and correct personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To submit a request, contact us using the information below and include “California Privacy Request” in the subject.

9. EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. You may also withdraw consent at any time for optional features (e.g., Last.fm). You can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date and, where appropriate, by providing in‑app notice.

11. Contact Us

For questions or privacy requests, contact: mail@tunetransit.app
